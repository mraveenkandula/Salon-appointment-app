Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make an appointment?

Yes, we operate by appointment only to ensure personalized attention and maintain our peaceful environment. You can book online 24/7 or call us during business hours.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Same-day cancellations may be subject to a 50% charge. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay).

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for any service or dollar amount. They make perfect gifts for birthdays, holidays, or special occasions. Contact us to purchase.

Are you really women-only?

Yes, our clinic is exclusively for women clients. This policy ensures a comfortable, private environment where you can fully relax and enjoy your treatments.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Just bring yourself! We provide all necessary supplies, robes, and towels. For spa treatments, you may want to avoid wearing makeup and bring flip-flops for pedicures.
